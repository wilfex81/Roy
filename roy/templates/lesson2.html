Lesson 2: Navigation and Checkpoints

Welcome back to the Road Rally Course! In this lesson, we'll be learning about navigation and checkpoints, which are essential for success in road rallies

First, let's talk about reading maps and plotting a course:

  • Learn how to read maps and understand the symbols and legends.
  • Practice plotting a course using a map and a route book.
  • Understand how to use GPS and other navigation tools effectively.

Now, let's cover the importance of checkpoints:

  • Understand what checkpoints are and why they are important in road rallies.
  • Learn how to navigate to checkpoints efficiently and accurately.
  • Understand how to use timing and speed control to reach checkpoints on time.

Note:

Driving is a serious responsibility, and it's important to take it seriously. If you have any doubts or concerns about your ability to drive safely, seek out additional training or resources to help you become a better driver.
