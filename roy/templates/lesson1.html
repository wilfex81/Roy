Lesson 1: Safety and Driving Basics

Welcome to the Road Rally Course! In this course, we'll be learning everything you need to know to become a skilled and safe driver. This first lesson will cover the basics of driving safety and driving basics.

Before we get started, let's review some basic safety rules:

  • Always wear your seatbelt.
  • Obey the speed limit and all traffic laws.
  • Never drive under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
  • Avoid using your phone or other distractions while driving.
  • Always keep a safe distance from other vehicles.

Now, let's cover some driving basics:

  • Adjust your mirrors and seat before you start driving.
  • Always use your turn signals to indicate your intentions.
  • Use the correct hand positions on the steering wheel.
  • Practice smooth acceleration and braking.
  • Always be aware of your surroundings and stay focused on the road.

Note:

Driving is a serious responsibility, and it's important to take it seriously. If you have any doubts or concerns about your ability to drive safely, seek out additional training or resources to help you become a better driver.
