DID YOU KNOW?

The popularity of road rallies dates back more than a century. Since 1895, when the first documented road rally was held in France, it has become a well-liked form of racing and leisure.

DID YOU KNOW?

The Paris-Dakar Rally, which travels thousands of kilometers through some of the world's most difficult terrain, is the most famous road rally in existence.

DID YOU KNOW?

Depending on the area and the event, road rallies may be staged on public or private property. Road rallies might involve competition or not. While some rallies are competitive timed events with competitors, others are straightforward tours or scavenger hunts.
